About "स्वध्येय फाऊंडेशन"

Namaste,

As we all know India is a developing country and similarly each and every state. Despite this fact, there are many weaker sections where people are still fighting for basic needs. Concerning this, we thought of doing something for the needy. And from this generosity "स्वध्येय" was born on 19th January, 2020 and it is officially established on 02nd November, 2020. "स्वध्येय" is basically a combination of two words, "स्व" which means "SELF" & "ध्येय" means “AIM".

"स्वध्येय" main core aspects are as bellows:
1) Unplugged Education,
2) Fly Together,
3) Conserve to preserve,
4) वारसा .

We are acting as a connection between the unprivileged people and their dreams. "स्वध्येय" have and will work on each and every ground for providing the help and support. We are fortunate that in this commitment for ours towards the society we have many well-wishers as our source of strength.

Vision of स्वध्येय :

Our vision is to help the needy, orphans, disabled. Also, we provide encouragement for those who wish to achieve big dreams, by giving required support and transform the minds of students towards self-employment with our learning activities. We are acting as an intermediatery between underprivileged and the society.

Mission of स्वध्येय :

Our mission is to work on the basic needs of human beings i.e. Food, Clothing, Shelter, Education and Health. We will help in all aspects and create a difference in our society.

By doing these small acts, we are trying to help and create a difference in our society.
