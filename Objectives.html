Fly Together

Our objective: is to extend genuine care and support to the underprivileged and needy individuals, as well as animals in society.

Focus Area: We primarily focus on aiding people and animals in marginalized areas such as roadsides, slums, and unreachable regions.

Initiatives: We conduct various impactful initiatives including awareness campaigns, cleaning campaigns, job opportunities, health camps, and blood donation camps to address the needs of the community.

Non-Discriminatory Assistance: We provide assistance without any bias based on caste, religion, or any other factors.

Beneficial Projects: We undertake projects that directly benefit the people and animals in society.

Join Us: We invite individuals and organizations to join us in our journey to empower, uplift, and make a lasting difference in the lives of those who need it the most. Together, we aim to build a compassionate and inclusive society.

Conserve To Preserve

When we talk about conserving nature, we're really talking about safeguarding our future. Nature provides vital resources for our survival and enjoyment. This includes living organisms and natural forces like weather, mountains, oceans, and landscapes.

Our project aims to raise awareness about nature conservation. We undertake activities like tree planting, promoting the mantra of "Reduce, Reuse, and Recycle," and supporting eco-friendly products. This is crucial for protecting our environment and securing our future for generations to come.

Our efforts include:

  • Conducting beach clean-ups and tree planting to promote conservation.
  • Highlighting the importance of environmental awareness for sustaining our planet.
  • Prioritizing initiatives for reusing and recycling to manage waste responsibly.
  • Placing awareness boards in corporate, café, and restaurant settings to advocate for water conservation and cultivate a conscious society dedicated to environmental well-being.

Through these actions, we strive to preserve nature and ensure a sustainable future for all.

Fort Conservation

Our project, वारसा, focuses on preserving and raising awareness about India's historical significance, particularly in Maharashtra. We aim to connect with people through conservation and awareness campaigns related to heritage or historical places to deepen appreciation for our rich heritage.

The research involves analyzing the current condition of places and other historical sites, followed by finalizing plans for their conservation. Working alongside volunteers and team members, we strive to achieve our goals on the ground.

Raising heritage awareness is crucial, requiring local support and team commitment. Activities that engage the community are vital for maintaining respect for our heritage.

Many historical sites have been neglected, leading to deterioration. It's essential to take action now to protect these valuable assets for future generations.

Educating and involving youth is key to preserving India's heritage. By respecting our ancestors and preserving our heritage, we honor our history and ensure a better future.
