We are "स्वध्येय फाऊंडेशन"

Namaste,

As we all know India is a developing country and similarly each and every state. Despite this fact, there are many weaker sections where people are still fighting for basic needs. Concerning this, we thought of doing something for the needy. And from this generosity "स्वध्येय" was born and it officially started on 19th January, 2020.

"स्वध्येय" is basically a combination of two words, "स्व" which means "SELF" & "ध्येय" means " AIM" or "IDEAL". Swadhey mainly aims at our 4 main objectives which are as follows: 1) Unplugged Education, 2) Helping Needy, 3) "वारसा" (Fort Conservation), 4) Conserve to preserve.

We are fortunate that in this commitment for ours towards the society we have many well-wishers as our source of strength.
