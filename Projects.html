Amrai Hostel

Swadhey have undertaken many projects another important from one of those is for boys from hostel of Kalyan. This hostel comes under Kalyan - Dombivli Municipal corporation. Approximately 30 to 35 boys stay there (from age group of 6 to 18) out of which some are orphans, some are single parent kid. We have visited the hostel few days back and met one of concern authority person and informed him about Swadhey. After visit we came to know that hostel have many difficulties even to meet their daily needs to.

Though this hostel comes under Government provisions there are many things required and hence we decided to start activities for that hostel. This campaign is so important for us because this is very first hostel campaign undertaken by Swadhey. We are going to conduct various activities that enforce the each moto of Swadhey. Whether it is financial help to the boys in this hostel, or sessions for their social and personality development, or to develop their skills Swadhey is trying to complete their every need. Swadhey needs your wishes and support. Also, the list of things which can be donated is given on our website. You need to click on donate now and select things as per your availability to donate. Your support will be highly appreciated!

This project will start soon

Online Education

With GYC (Geolife Youth Club) it's OUR FIRST COLLABORATION**

As They say TEAMWORK MAKES THE DREAM WORK. So Swadhey along with Geolife Youth Club has started A Project of Teaching and developing, reading and writing skills in English. Through this project we are focusing on our wise vision to make a better tomorrow. On 6th December,2020 the first session was held with the students from Thane and Badlapur Orphanage.

Following will be our motive for conducting session further sessions:

  • To improve their English language skills.
  • Focus on basics first, so that they get a hang of the language.
  • Include activities, interaction, group discussions etc in order to make learning fun.
  • To work on overall personality development.
  • We plan to add sessions pertaining to health, nutrition etc

And Here we are guys...We have succesfully taken our first step towards education for all. We are grateful for GYC team for their valueable time and cooperation.

Start at 09.00 AM (Every Sunday)

Helping Hands (Ulhasnagar)

A number of initiatives are being implemented under the Swadhey Foundation. At the same time, planning is in motion on many important issues. One of them was, the desire to do something for the children of poor families or for those who could not learn despite their love for education and this initiative can be marked as a beginning.

This project is about Priya Shinde and her adolescent daughters. This family is located in Ulhasnagar-a Mumbai suburb. The family consists of parents and 4 young daughters is having numerous problems. An Addicted father, Financial Crisis and a daily fight to bring atleast a single meal of the day to a plate. When we found them they were in devastating situation.The place was completely flooded with tons of dirt, mosquitoes, was stinking.

According to our vision, we don't just help people who need help..but also help people who want to help themselves. With that in mind, SWADHEY team had a talk with the lady(Mother). Then it was decided that in the next 2 days, the family would be moved to another better place and will be provided with basic help and education to the little girls.

The situation of this family was revealed to us by one of our friend, Bhavesh .We really want to thank him for his wonderful selfless deed. With such support and help of the people like Bhavesh, there is no doubt that such great deeds will be done in the future as well. Special thanks to Yari Foundation

According to our vision, we don't just help people who need help..but also help people who want to help themselves. With that in mind, SWADHEY team had a talk with the Father.
