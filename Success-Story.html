Success Story

Empowering Lives: The Suryawanshi Success Story


Introduction:
We Meet Bhaskar Suryawanshi and his three resilient sons from Ambernath, a Mumbai suburb. Faced with an addicted father and financial crisis, their daily struggle for a single meal seemed insurmountable.

Discovery and Intervention:
In alignment with our vision, the SWADHEY team engaged with the family. Recognizing the potential for change, a decision was made to support them with basic necessities and provide education for the boys. Consequently, the boys were welcomed into the care of Jeevan Samvardhan Foundation.

Transformation Journey:
Since the discovery on 15/11/2020, a comprehensive transformation began. Legal documentation and the consent of family members, police, and Jeevan Samvardhan Foundation facilitated the admission of the three children to JSF on 24/12/2020.

Educational Milestones:
The Suryawanshi boys flourished under JSF's care. Soham Suryawanshi, at 14 years in 8th standard, achieved state-level recognition in Jharkhand for his prowess in karate and kickboxing. Mohan Suryawanshi, aged 13 in 7th standard, excelled as a karate expert. Pavan Suryawanshi, 15 years old in 9th standard, showcased proficiency in kickboxing and attained an A+ in elementary drawing.

A Beacon of Hope:
This success story embodies our belief in not just helping those in need but empowering individuals who strive to help themselves. The Suryawanshi family's journey from adversity to achievement exemplifies the transformative impact of collective efforts.

Join us in celebrating this remarkable success story of resilience, determination, and empowerment.
