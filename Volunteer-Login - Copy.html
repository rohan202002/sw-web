Terms & Conditions

For Volunteers :
  • Your Responsibilities - To know your responsibilities towards Swadhey.
  • Privacy - To maintain privacy about Swadhey activities.
  • Data security & Monitoring - To keep Swadhey data secured.
  • Expected Behaviour - To maintain good conduct in society while serving people.
  • Be Kind - To be kind to everyone.
  • Volunteers Role - To participate in all Swadhey activities as much as you can.
For Membership :
  • Membership fee - To become a member you have to pay Rs.250/- per month for the next 6 Consecutive months. If you want to continue further, you can renew the membership for next 6 months.
  • Contribution - Members can contribute to Swadhey or donate to needy every month either by cash or kind
  • Members Role - Every members is needed to take active participation in all activities carried by Swadhey.
  • Rights - You will be having rights to vote in Swadhey decisions making & right to suggest for activities in Swadhey which will take Swadhey towards achieving goals to serve needy.
