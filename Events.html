Durg Dipotsav at Pimplas Fort

Title: :- Durg Dipotsav at Pimplas Fort

Continuing our cherished tradition, Swadhey Foundation celebrated Durg Dipotsav at the historic Pimplas Fort, blending the spirit of Diwali with our commitment to heritage preservation. This annual event reflects our deep respect for Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj, as we light the first diya in his honor before illuminating our homes.

The day began with a fort-cleaning drive, where our team actively worked to restore the fort’s surroundings, ensuring it retained its historical charm. Beautiful rangolis were drawn, and floral decorations added a vibrant touch to the site. As the morning sun rose, the fort radiated with the warm glow of traditional oil lamps, creating a serene and sacred ambiance.

Durg Dipotsav is not just a festival; it is a tribute to our rich cultural heritage and a reminder of our responsibility to preserve it for future generations. We extend our heartfelt gratitude to all the participants who contributed their time and effort to make this event meaningful and memorable.

A Fight for Survival- Puppies Medical Treatment

Title: :- A Fight for Survival- Puppies Medical Treatment

A heartbreaking case came to our attention — 7 street puppies infected with the parvo virus, a highly contagious and deadly disease. Without urgent treatment, their chances were slim. Swadhey Foundation immediately stepped in, admitting them to Pet Cure & Care Vet Clinic, where they received intensive care over five days.

Despite all efforts, we sadly lost one brave soul. But with love, dedication, and your timely donations, 6 puppies were saved and began their journey to recovery.

This was more than just a rescue — it was a fight for survival, and your support made all the difference. Thank you for being part of this life-saving mission.
