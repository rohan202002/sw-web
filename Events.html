2022

Ayurvedic Health Camp, Kalyan

Swadhey Foundation has an objective of Helping Hand under which we conduct various camps and campaigns which help the needy. Under the helping hand, one of the important aspects is Health. As we have mentioned in our mission statement, 'we work on the basic needs of Human Beings'. With the same motive, Toptime India Consumer Pvt Ltd Company organizes various health camps for the people. We got to know about these camps from one of our volunteers and we reached out to them. When we met them they also wanted to conduct camps with the help of Swadhey foundation, and then we needed to set a date for camp and decided that let’s start the year with Ayurvedic Health Camp.

On the occasion of the New Year, we have organized Ayurvedic Health Camp for all the people on 1st and 2nd of January, 2022 in Kalyan East. 80+ people have been checked up and consulted by Dr. Dharmendra Dixit (MD in Naturopathy) and Miss. Vidya Ahire. This was our first ever health camp under the objective of Helping Hands. Soon we are organizing more health camps for the needy ones.

This event is closed

Shree Datta Tarun Mandal, Kalyan (E)
