Distribution of Diwali Ank

"Diwali Ank" is another concept like the "Durg Dipostav". Swadhey Foundation always follows the uniqueness in every work they took in hand. Diwali Ank is the concept of a normal booklet which is given in schools or available in any book stores. These are basically named as Diwali Ank because it was meant to solve in Diwali vacation. It includes education related activities like drawing, general knowledge, information, and reading. It helps the children to be more competent and use their brains in solving such booklets.

In Swadhey Foundation's Diwali ank, we named it as "गरुडझेप", which means take a jump like Eagle and fly above all. As you know, An Eagle is a bird that flies really high in the sky and no one can fly above the eagle's height. So we had to name it like children who are solving the Diwali Ank of Swadhey Foundation, must try to take a jump (Try his best) to fly on Eagle's level or beyond that (To achieve the target which was set by you). In overall meaning, we wanted the children to get inspired by our Diwali Ank.

The whole design, content and preparation were done by, the specially qualified teachers in schools. We make Diwali Ank for 1st to 10th standard students. This year we distributed it to our Jeevan Samvardhan Foundation and many people took it from us for their children.

Distribution of Diwali Ank

"Diwali Ank" is another concept like the "Durg Dipostav". Swadhey Foundation always follows the uniqueness in every work they took in hand. Diwali Ank is the concept of a normal booklet which is given in schools or available in any book stores. These are basically named as Diwali Ank because it was meant to solve in Diwali vacation. It includes education related activities like drawing, general knowledge, information, and reading. It helps the children to be more competent and use their brains in solving such booklets.

In Swadhey Foundation's Diwali ank, we named it as "गरुडझेप", which means take a jump like Eagle and fly above all. As you know, An Eagle is a bird that flies really high in the sky and no one can fly above the eagle's height. So we had to name it like children who are solving the Diwali Ank of Swadhey Foundation, must try to take a jump (Try his best) to fly on Eagle's level or beyond that (To achieve the target which was set by you). In overall meaning, we wanted the children to get inspired from our Diwali Ank.

The whole design, content and preparation were done by the specially qualified teachers in schools. This year we made two separate Diwali Ank based on our last years experience. One for junior students and another for above 3rd standards till 8th. For students above 8th standard, we made a list of tasks or activities which helped them to build a very strong base for further education. This year we distributed it to our Jeevan Samvardhan Foundation and many people took it from us for their children.
