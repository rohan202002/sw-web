►Reviving & Restoring Nature

(Environmental Conservation Initiatives)

We are dedicated to protecting and rejuvenating the environment through impactful actions like beach cleanups and tree plantations, fostering a cleaner and greener future.

Beach Cleanup Drives

In 2022, we launched the Arnala Beach Cleaning Project to restore the coastal environment.

Through eight impactful drives, volunteers removed significant amounts of waste while raising awareness among locals and tourists.

Dustbins were distributed, encouraging responsible waste disposal. This initiative thrived in collaboration with Arnala Social Work Group, a long-standing advocate for beach conservation.

This collective effort not only revived the beach’s beauty but also fostered a lasting sense of environmental responsibility.

Impact: Cleaner beaches, increased awareness, and active community participation.

Together for Nature. Together for Tomorrow.

► Honoring Legacy, Inspiring Change

(Cultural & Awareness Campaigns)

Celebrating our rich heritage and inspiring change through meaningful tributes, conservation efforts, and awareness initiatives that honor history and shape the future.

Chatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Jayanti

Every year, we celebrate Shiv Jayanti by distributing snacks and water to modern-day Mavalas—the dedicated policemen who tirelessly serve and protect us.

Over the past four years, we have distributed around 300-400 snack boxes and 500 water bottles annually across Maharashtra as a token of gratitude for their service.

Impact:  Fostered appreciation for law enforcement and honored Maharaj's legacy of bravery.

Durg Dipotsav

Durg Dipotsav is our initiative to honor Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj by celebrating at historical forts. Activities include cleaning fort surroundings, creating rangolis, and lighting traditional Diwali diyas, first at Maharaj’s statue and then at home.

This campaign highlights the importance of fort conservation and heritage preservation.

Impact:  Increased awareness about heritage conservation while spreading cultural values.

Fort Conservation Activity

We conducted cleanup drives at Dronagiri Fort in Uran, focusing on maintaining these historical landmarks and spreading awareness about responsible tourism.

Impact: Preservation of historical sites and strengthened public involvement in conservation.

Other Awareness Campaigns

- Rakhi for Rakshak: We gathered handwritten letters and rakhis from the public, sending them to soldiers as an expression of gratitude during Raksha Bandhan, fostering a sense of connection and appreciation for our nation's defenders.

- Independence Day Celebrations: Celebrated in various hostels and NGOs, fostering patriotism and educating orphaned children about the significance of freedom.

- Tribute to Great Leaders: Commemorated the birth anniversaries of influential leaders in orphanages, sharing their life stories and contributions to society to inspire the younger generation.
